Se suele cantar como: “Sol solecito hace frio, sal y caliéntame no seas malito”.

For everyone it represents a new dawn to begin, a new time to fix the things that went wrong. Perhaps the sun of the Mexican lottery has represented very important things since ancient times, which is why the Mayans used it to manage time. In colonial times the sun was the clock to measure the hours and the space between days.
